The Story Of La Pino'z Pizza

The story of India's fastest growing pizza place started back in 2011 when Sanam Kapoor opened his very first pizzeriain his home town Chandigarh. Inspired by his own searchfor delicious pizza options, he started to wonder if there was a better way of doing pizza and business. The last thing the world want was another pizeria, but maybe this one could be different and everyone could get exactly what they wanted, fresh on demand. It all started sounding pretty great, and soon Sanam was planning to start to open first La Pino'z in his hometown Chandigarh and there was no look back. As of now La Pino'z has 600+ outlets across India and a recent one opening in London.
